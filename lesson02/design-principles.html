Design Principles Showcase

Spotify

Spotify's mobile homepage exemplifies Hick's Law by keeping the navigation options limited and clear. The five large, labeled icons in the top bar make it easy for users to find what they need without being overwhelmed by too many choices. This reduces decision fatigue and improves user experience.

YouTube

YouTube's mobile homepage applies Fitt's Law by making the play button for the featured video large and central. This prime location and ample size make it easy for users to tap and start watching, minimizing the time and effort required to reach the desired action.

Visit YouTube

Apple

Apple's mobile product pages utilize the Rule of Thirds by prominently displaying the hero image in two-thirds of the screen, off-center to add dynamism. The key features and call to action are then positioned at the intersections of the thirds, drawing the user's eye to the most important information and action point.
